Easy burger recipe


Home!

A clasic american dish and very delicious

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patties
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Ketchup & mustard
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Season the beef patties with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or skillet.
  3. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  4. Assemble with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and sauces.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your burger!
