Lasagna - My favorite dish


Home!

Descrition

Delicious homemade dish under 45 minutes!

Clasic homemade ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic & Onion
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook beef with garlic and onion.
  3. Mix ricotta with salt and pepper.
  4. Layer noodles, meat, ricotta, and cheeses.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30 minutes.
